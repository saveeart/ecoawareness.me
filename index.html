Informatin About The Middle East

The Middle East is a culturally diverse and historically rich region spanning Western Asia and parts of North Africa. It is known for its ancient civilizations, religious significance, and vibrant cities, blending tradition with modernity across deserts, seas, and mountains.

Ancient Cities

The Middle East, known as the "cradle of civilization," is home to some of the world's oldest cities, including Damascus, Baghdad, and Jerusalem, each with histories spanning thousands of years.

Region rich in oil.

The Middle East holds vast oil reserves, with some of the world's largest deposits. This oil wealth has significantly shaped global politics and economies, making the region a key player in energy markets.

Culturally and architecturally diverse

The Middle East boasts diverse cultural and architectural landmarks, from ancient Persepolis to modern Dubai. Home to various ethnic groups, it's renowned for vibrant souks, rich cuisine, and warm hospitality.

Our Mission

Through EcoAwareness, our mission is to protect the Middle East's historical sites from the damaging effects of
climate change by three ways:

Public Awarness:

We aim to raise public awareness about the real effects of climate change through various channels, including in-person events and social media platforms like Instagram, to educate and inspire positive action.

Posting our Research:

This website provides comprehensive information on relevant scientific research about climate change, offering insights into its causes, effects, and potential solutions to help you stay informed and take meaningful action.

Adopting Sustainable Practices:

Encourage communities and individuals to reduce their carbon footprint by conserving energy, adopting sustainable consumption habits, reducing waste, and supporting the transition to renewable energy sources.
